As a Family Nurse Practitioner, I see patients every day who come to appointments feeling unprepared — unsure what questions to ask, unable to recall their medication history, or uncertain about what screenings they're due for. WellCharted was born from that gap. These tools aren't meant to replace your provider — they're designed to make your relationship with your provider more productive, more organized, and more confident. Every tracker, guide, and worksheet in the WellCharted library is built around one goal: helping you and your family walk into any medical appointment ready to advocate for yourselves. ⚠️ Important Note All WellCharted tools are for personal health organization and communication purposes only. They do not replace the diagnosis, advice, or treatment of a licensed healthcare provider. Always consult your primary care provider regarding your health.
